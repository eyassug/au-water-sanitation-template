Welcome

Template for National Need Assessment
for preparing the Kagame Action Plan for operationalizing the January 2014 African Union
Assembly Decision AU/DEC. 516 (XXII) on the implementation of the July 2008 Sharm EI-Sheikh
Commitments for Accelerating the Achievement of Water and Sanitation Goals in Africa.

The current template intends to compile information related to the country national actions programming for water supply and sanitation in rural and urban poor settlements for the coming years, while highlighting the anticipated gaps for the year 2015 for reaching the MDGs targets in specific Priority Areas. Priority Area in this document refers to a geographical setting classified as rural or urban poor (periurban) that is controlled by a Municipality or any related national Authority. Focus of the gap assessment is placed on the existing technologies, the management, institutional and funding approaches that are already in use for the communities. The Kagame Action Plan will base on those gaps and the identified strengths and weaknesses of the implementation of the national water and sanitation programmes, to define necessary interventions to quickly close the anticipated gaps for the end 2015. {% endblock %}
